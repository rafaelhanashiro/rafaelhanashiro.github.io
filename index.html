About Me

Hello, my name is Rafael Hanashiro. I'm a 19 year old Java developer from Brazil. Currently, I am a Computer Science major at the Georgia Institute of Technology, expecting to graduate in 2020.

I am a current participant in Georgia Tech's Idea To Prototype (I2P) program, which provides funding for students to develop prototypes of their own ideas. I am currently designing an autonomous delivery drone, which provides faster and more efficient delivery in emergency situations.

I am also involved in the Brazilian Student Association at Georgia Tech, an association for Brazilian students studying in the US, and in the Society of Hispanic Engineers, an organization for Hispanic engineers.
